Essentials

Browse my publications and curriculum vitae.

My main research interests are:

  • network science,
  • graph algorithms,
  • parameterized complexity,
  • approximation algorithms,
  • computational geometry, and
  • complexity theory.

Recently

(last updated: May 2025)

  • Program committee co-chair for IPEC 2025.
  • Paper accepted at WG 2025: Concurrency Constrained Scheduling with Tree-Like Constraints, with Hans Bodlaender and Danny Hermelin. (arxiv version)
  • Program committee member for COCOON 2025.
  • Invited speaker at SOFSEM 2025. Invited paper in the proceedings on Open Problems and Recent Developments on a Complexity Framework for Forbidden Subgraphs.
  • PhD student Sukanya Pandey defended her thesis "Cutting-Edge Algorithms for Monotone Graph Classes".
  • Best paper award at ISAAC 2024 for paper Complexity Framework for Forbidden Subgraphs II: Edge Subdivision and the "H"-Graphs with Vadim Lozin, Barnaby Martin, Sukanya Pandey, Daniel Paulusma, Mark Siggers, and Siani Smith.
  • Paper accepted to Algorithmica: Parameterized Complexity of Streaming Diameter and Connectivity Problems, with Jelle Oostveen.
